How to use a payment agent?

You can use an authorized payment agent to process deposits and withdrawals for payment methods or local currencies not supported by Binary.com. It's easy to do.

  1. Check out the payment methods accepted by the payment agents in the list below.
  2. Choose the payment method you'd like to use.
  3. Contact the payment agent directly for more information on how to transfer money to them.
  4. Send your funds to the payment agent. The deposited funds, less the agent's commission, will show up in your account.
